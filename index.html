♛ Wedding Planning

Service 01

Wedding Planning

Our comprehensive wedding planning service is the pinnacle of our offerings. We handle every intricate detail — from conceptualizing your theme to coordinating vendors, timelines, and logistics — allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the joy of your celebration.

  • Complete event coordination & management
  • Personalized theme conceptualization
  • Venue selection and site management
  • Vendor sourcing, negotiation & coordination
  • Day-of coordination & emergency planning
  • Budget management & timeline creation
✈ Destinations

Service 02

Destination Weddings

Take your love story to breathtaking locations across Kerala's backwaters, hill stations, and beaches, or venture beyond to exotic destinations worldwide. We handle every logistical detail, transforming any location into your perfect paradise.

  • Location scouting & venue securing
  • Guest accommodation & transportation
  • Legal & permit coordination
  • Local vendor management
  • Cultural experience integration
  • Full travel coordination for wedding party
❧ Decoration

Service 03

Decoration & Styling

Transform your venue into a visual masterpiece. Our creative stylists craft immersive environments that tell your love story through flowers, fabric, lighting, and artful design — creating moments that take your breath away at every turn.

  • Floral design & arrangement
  • Mandap/altar design & setup
  • Ambient & statement lighting design
  • Table styling & centrepiece creation
  • Photo backdrop & photo booth design
  • Entrance décor & ceiling installations
☽ Catering

Service 04

Catering Coordination

A feast worthy of royalty. We partner with Kerala's finest culinary masters to create extraordinary dining experiences that blend traditional flavors with contemporary presentation, ensuring every guest savors an unforgettable meal.

  • Menu curation & tasting sessions
  • Multi-cuisine options & dietary accommodations
  • Premium bar & beverage service
  • Elegant table settings & service staff
  • Wedding cake design & coordination
  • Live cooking stations & food experiences
